THE FOUNDER

Nithyananthan Nagarajan

Nithyananthan Nagarajan founded NSK Groups with a clear conviction — that building great technology companies requires systems thinking, not solo execution.

NSK Groups was architected from day one as a group structure: each venture operates independently, serves a distinct market, and is built to scale beyond any single founder.
